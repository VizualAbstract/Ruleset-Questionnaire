Shipping Interval

Do you want orders on a month, week, or day cycle?

Day of Week/Month

Do you want to ship on a particular day of week or month? For example, on the 15th of the month, or every Thursday?

Sync schedule

If the customer places an order on the 12th and the re-order date is scheduled for the 15th, will you want the initial order on the 12th sent out in addition to the scheduled order on the 15th? If the customer places an order on the Monday and the re-order day is scheduled for Thursday, will you want the initial order on Monday sent out in addition to the scheduled order on Thursday?

Note: We immediatly send the first order to your Shopify store after a customer finishes the checkout, regardless of schedule.

Pre-Payments

Do you want to have a customer pay for more than one shipment at a time?

If yes: enter a charge frequency that is longer than the order frequency.

Expiration

Finally, Do you want the subscription expire after a set number of charges.

If yes: click expiration and enter number of charges until expiration. If you enter zero or 1 it automatically expires after the first charge.

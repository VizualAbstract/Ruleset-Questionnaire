Day of Week/Month

Do you want to ship on a particular day of week or month? For example, on the 15th of the month, or every Thursday?

Sync schedule

If the customer places an order on the 12th and the re-order date is scheduled for the 15th, will you want the re-order scheduled for the 15th to proceed as normal? If the customer places an order on the Monday and the re-order day is scheduled for Thursday, will you want the re-order scheduled for Tuesday to proceed as normal?

Note: We immediatly send the first order to your Shopify store after a customer finishes the checkout, regardless of schedule.

Pre-Payments

Do you want to have a customer pay for more than one shipment at a time? For example, charging every 4 weeks, or every 2 months.

If yes: enter a charge frequency that is longer than the order frequency.

Expiration

Do you want the subscription expire after a set number of charges.

If yes: click expiration and enter number of charges until expiration. If you enter zero or 1 it automatically expires after the first charge.
